Payment terms for Teachers & Students:

  1. No charges for opening Teacher/Student account. It's completely free.
  2. One time Teacher account activation price is Rs. 1000/- (One thousand rupees only)
  3. Teacher have completely freedom to set price and duration for each separate topic/course of his/her expertise as per Teacher's wish. Each topic or course will be listed on LearnTEZ platform separate as an individual product.
  4. The Teacher set price & duration for particular topic/course is directly visible student in searchable product database. (respective tax will be applicable on this price and charged to students)
  5. LearnTEZ will collect fees from student at the time of booking or enrollment to the topic/course. Such fees collected by the LearnTEZ will be transferred to wallet facility available on our website and application within 24 hrs after successful execution of the session. Electronic system generated invoice will be sent to student and teacher on their registered Email ID also it can be viewed under 'WALLET/My Wallet' tab and booking can be visible under 'BOOKINGS/ My Bookings' tab on LearnTEZ app and website.
  6. LearnTEZ will deduct its service charge from the fees collected by student during booking/ enrollment to topic/course. Service charge consist of percentage of the fees collected by LearnTEZ from student.
  7. LearnTEZ service charge is 20% of individual product (Topic/course) price set by Teacher. In case of Group session/Topic/Course having recurring or more than one session in one booking, then payment for individual session is calculated on pro-rata basis and released on to teacher wallet after successful execution of every session until the successful execution of final session of that booking.
  8. Minimum wallet balance requirement is 100 Rs. Teachers/Students are able to withdraw amount more than 100 Rs. From his wallet balance. (Minimum Wallet balance will be refunded to Teacher/Student on account cancellation request sent on admin@learntez.com)
  9. To withdraw money from LearnTEZ Teacher's wallet to bank account, Teacher has to raise withdraw request from website/ application only. Payment related requests through telephonic conversations are not considered in anyway.
  10. Teacher's/Student's raise withdraw request will be processed on weekly basis on every Thursday and Teachers/Students can expect money to their bank account within 3 working days. Applicable (3% ) transactional charge will be applicable on amount withdraw to bank account by Teacher.
  11. Please note that if a Teacher/Student payment sent by LearnTEZ to you in accordance with these Teacher/Student terms of service is later invalidated or reversed for any reason (e.g. A student requests a refund), you will be liable to LearnTEZ for the full amount of such Teacher Payment that LearnTEZ sent to you and any fees related there to. This means that you will be responsible for reimbursing LearnTEZ in the event of a chargeback, reversal of payment or other claim by a student in this regard. You will also be responsible for the applicable service charge if you fail to provide lesson services that you previously agreed to provide to a student, and LearnTEZ is forced thereby to return the Teacher payment to the student. You agree that any amounts owed by you to LearnTEZ may be deducted directly from future Teacher payments to you.
  12. All service charges/refund/cancellation percentage can be change at any point of time without prior notice. All rights reserved with LearnTEZ.

Cancellation terms for Teachers:

  1. If a requested enrolment/booking is cancelled before 4hrs of start of session (before any sessions are provided), any amounts collected by LearnTEZ will be refunded to such Student. No any charges will be applicable in this case. (Teachers/Students cannot cancel session bookings within 4hrs before session start time)
  2. If Teacher/student failed to attend the session it will be considered as absentee and 10 % of service charge will be deducted from product price in case of student and from wallet in case of teacher. The remaining amount will be refunded to student.
  3. If teacher/student failed to attend the session due to not meeting the minimum system requirement for execution of session the sole responsibility lies with teacher/student. In such case no refund will be provided.
  4. If in case of technical failure or website down time LearnTEZ will refund any amount collected for that session during booking.
  5. Tax collected once will not be refunded in any case if service is availed/cancelled by Teacher/Student.
  6. In case of student failed to attend the session and Teacher attend the session 5% of product price will only be credited to Teacher wallet.
  7. In case of student failed to attend the session and Teacher attend the session 85% of product price will only be credited to student wallet.
  8. In case of Teacher failed to attend the session and student attend the session 10% of product price will be debited from Teacher wallet.
  9. In case of Teacher failed to attend the session and student attend the session 100% of product price will be credited to student wallet.
  10. In case of both Teacher and student are absent 10% of product price from student and 5 % of product price from Teachers wallet balance will be debited as service charge.
  11. Tax collected once will not be refunded in any case if service is availed/cancelled or absentee in session by Teacher/Student.
  12. In case of both Teacher and student are present 80% of product price from student will be credited to Teachers wallet.
  13. In case of topic/course cancellation by Teacher or student in between of topic/course having recurring or more than one session in one booking, then cancellation charges are applicable to unconsumed portion of topic/course on pro-rata basis. And refund will be made according to above clauses 1 to 12 whichever is applicable.
  14. All transactions and changes are visible to Teacher/student wallet within 24 hrs after session end time.
  15. All service charges/refund/cancellation percentage can be change at any point of time without prior notice. All rights reserved with LearnTEZ.

Refund terms for Teachers:

  1. LearnTEZ Refund window is of 30 days from the date you purchased LearnTEZ Teacher account activation.
  2. If a request is submitted online through email for immediate cancellation within the first 30 days, a full refund will be issued. (email to: admin@LearnTEZ.com)
  3. Teacher's refund request will be processed on weekly basis on every Thursday and Teachers can expect money to their bank account within 3 working days. Applicable (3% ) transactional charge will be applicable on amount withdraw to bank account by Teacher.
  4. LearnTEZ will not process refund requests received after the refund window.
